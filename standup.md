# Stand Up Meeting Template

## Meeting Logistics

### Date & Time

Insert the date (MM/DD/YYYY), start time (##:## AM/PM), and end time (##:## AM/PM) here.

### Participants

Insert the participants of the meeting here.

### Meeting Type

If applicable, describe the type of meeting here (1-on-1 check-in meeting, group check-in meeting, recurring meeting, etc.).

## Meeting Contents

### Pre-Meeting Content

Include content and materials prior but relevant to the meeting. This can include but is not limited to images, videos, and other sources that may be required as context for this current meeting.

### Meeting Purpose

Include the purpose of this meeting, which is likely to be worded as a statement of what we will come out of this meeting with (a skill, a plan, etc.)

### Post-Meeting Content

If relevant, and differs from the meeting purpose, describe content that may wish to be discussed in future meetings (possibly due to lack of time in this meeting, or a different topic that was not addressed).

## Post-Assignments

Add any assignments that may be wished to be completed after the meeting and before the next upcoming meeting.

- If necessary/useful, place assignments in these bullet points.

## Post-Notes

If applicable, place any meeting notes here that may need to be addressed (a fellow employee was sick and could not attend, so there needs to be communication with them).
